"Her music is not just happy or sad"

Karlijn Langendijk (1995) composes all the intricate shades of emotions that people feel really intensely but cannot communicate in words.

As a guitarist & composer, she is fascinated by all kinds of different music genres and she mixes all these together into a completely unique style.
