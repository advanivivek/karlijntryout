She has shared the stage with musicians like Tommy Emmanuel and Peter Finger & toured in Germany, Italy, China and India.

Karlijn Langendijk (1995) grew up in the Netherlands surrounded by guitar music at home. She started playing the guitar when she was 13 years old, inspired by her Father, who was a great guitarist himself.

In November 2015 she won the first prize and the audience prize at the “SAI Acoustic Guitar Competition” in the Czech Republic.

In 2017 Karlijn graduated from the Conservatory in Utrecht (NL) and started with her master studies in “Acoustic Guitar” at the Hochschule für Musik in Dresden, Germany.

K O B L E R  G U I T A R S

This nylon crossover guitar sounds both acoustically and amplified very natural and powerful. Because of the crossover model, the playability is great for modern fingerstyle as well as classical pieces. The compact body and cut-away give it a nice feminine touch, and the spruce top and santos rosewood back and sides make it a perfect instrument to also play percussion on.
The pickup system inside is a Fishman Matrix Infinity piezo & a Miniflex Model 1 microphone.
All these features make Kobler Guitars very unique instruments. If you're interested, you can check out Christina Kobler's other work here.

V O S S  G U I T A R S

My newest guitar is this wonderful semi-baritone steelstring guitar built by Alexander Voss-Schütte from Voss Guitars.
This was love at first 'touch' ! I've always wanted a steelstring baritone, but could never find one that fits my music and playing style; until the moment I met this beautiful beast in September 2017. The basses are very deep and the melody stands out beautifully. Because the size of this baritone is not as big as the usual baritone guitars, it's very comfortable for me to play it in any style I want; fast songs, slow ballads & percussion.
Videos with this guitar will be coming out soon on my YouTube channel!

B A T O N  R O U G E  G U I T A R S

The quality and playability of these guitars is amazing! And not just their 'usual guitars' like steelstrings and nylon crossovers, but also their electric jazz guitar, blues resonator or this cute guitarlele. A C U S  A M P S

My Acus One-8 Simon amp is the best way to amplify all my different guitars I'm playing on stage. Nylon string guitars, as well as steelstrings, sound very natural and powerful at the same time. With 3 inputs, lots of settings and even some great reverb possibilities, this is an essential part of my sound on stage!
